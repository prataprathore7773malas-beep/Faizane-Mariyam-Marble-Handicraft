ABOUT US

Creating Elegant Marble Masterpieces With Precision & Craftsmanship

Faizane Mariyam Marble Handicraft is a trusted manufacturer based in Makrana, Rajasthan, specializing in marble temples, fountains, handicrafts and custom marble creations.

With a commitment to quality craftsmanship, premium materials and customer satisfaction, we transform natural marble into timeless works of art for homes, gardens, religious places and commercial projects.

From traditional marble temples to modern decorative fountains and handcrafted marble products, every creation is carefully designed to deliver beauty, durability and elegance.
